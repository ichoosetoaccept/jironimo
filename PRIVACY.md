# Privacy Policy for Jironimo

**Last updated:** November 29, 2025

## Overview

Jironimo is a browser extension that helps you copy your Jira session cookie for use with command-line tools.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any data.**

## What the Extension Accesses

- **Cookies**: The extension reads the `JSESSIONID` cookie from the currently active tab when you click the extension icon. This cookie value is only copied to your clipboard when you explicitly click the "Copy" button.
- **Active Tab URL**: The extension reads the URL of your current tab to determine which domain to look for cookies on.

## Data Storage

- No data is stored by the extension
- No data is sent to any server
- Cookie values are only copied to your local clipboard

## Permissions Explained

- `cookies`: Required to read the JSESSIONID cookie from Jira
- `activeTab`: Required to detect the current tab's URL
